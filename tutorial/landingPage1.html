Chord Master

Hello, welcome to Chord Master. Chord Master is a one of a kind application that will become your best music-building friend. Do you have chords in mind that sound boring or plain? Have you ever heard the major triad of C4? What about the dominant ninth of a B2? Have you ever heard them together to see if they make a beautiful melody? Well now you can, with Chord Master.

If you don't really know too much about music theory, or just want some more information about how/what the different buttons do, I suggest the tips page!

Tips!

If you know a good amount of music theory, have seen the app before, or just want to dive into the application immediately, then go straight to the piano!
