Tutorial

  • A key refers to a piano key. Our Piano begins on the C3 key and ends on B6 key.
  • A chord is a combination of notes played together to create a rich harmony.
  • A Chord Family is a classification that different chords can fall under.
  • A Chord Inversion is a modification on a chord that changes which note is the lowest in that chord.
  • A Chord Progression is a sequence of chords put together.

The Piano

  • The default chord on our Piano is the Major C Triad. You may notice that this chord is displayed on the key above
  • To hear this chord, simply click on either the chord symbol, or any C on the piano.
  • You may have noticed that when you press a key on the piano, other keys that you havn't pressed become highlighted. These other highlighted keys are the other notes that belong in your selected chord.

Chord Family

  • Clicking on chord type button allows you to switch to the chord family that you want.
  • As you click around the different chord families, the chord that you have last clicked will be the chord that is next used
  • We have 17 different types of Chord Families that you are able to use and play around with.
  • Experiment with different different chords to try and achieve the right sound that you like!

Inversion

  • Clicking on the chord inversion drop down, brings up a menu that has labels from "None" through "Third"
  • Clicking on an inversion will change which note is the lowest note in the chord.

Progression

  • A chord progression is a sequence of chords. Add any chord you want into the sequence and play through it!
  • Once you have played the chord you would like to add it will automatically be added to the slot you have selected.
  • To add more chords into your progression click the + button. It will autofill any new chord with a C Major Triad.
  • If you want to copy any chord click the copy button to the bottom right of the chord.
  • If you want to delete a chord click the delete button to the top left of the chord.
  • To play through your progression click the play button in the top right of the screen.
  • If you want to restart, press the trash icon in the right corner and you will start from scratch at C3 Major Triad.
